About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the people behind Arya Samaj of Long Island

Our Mission

Extending Vedic Heritage for the Hindu Community

At Arya Samaj of Long Island, Inc., our mission is to preserve and promote the timeless wisdom of the Vedas for the benefit of the Hindu community and beyond. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where individuals of all backgrounds can connect with their spiritual roots, deepen their understanding of Vedic philosophy, and embrace the transformative power of ancient wisdom in their modern lives.

Our Vision

A World Enriched by Vedic Wisdom

We envision a world where the principles of the Vedas – truth, righteousness, peace, and universal brotherhood – are not merely studied but lived. Our goal is to be a catalyst for individual and collective transformation, inspiring people to embrace the timeless wisdom of the Vedas and apply it to create a more harmonious and enlightened society.

Our Values

Guiding Principles That Shape Our Service

Vedic Knowledge

We believe in the profound and practical wisdom of the Vedas as a source of guidance and inspiration for all aspects of life.

Community Harmony

We are committed to fostering unity, understanding, and cooperation within the Hindu community and beyond, promoting interfaith dialogue and respect for all.

Spiritual Growth

We encourage personal and collective spiritual evolution, providing opportunities for individuals to connect with their inner selves, cultivate a deeper understanding of Vedic principles, and live a more meaningful and purpose-driven life.
