About Arya Samaj of Long Island, Inc.

Preserving and Extending Vedic Heritage

Arya Samaj of Long Island, Inc. was formed on June 17, 2006, with the objective to extend the Vedic Heritage for the benefit of the Hindu Community at large. Our organization was established through the efforts of active members from Havan Groups operating in Nassau and Suffolk counties of Long Island, New York.

We are dedicated to preserving and promoting Vedic traditions, fostering spiritual growth, and serving the Hindu community in Long Island and beyond.
